About EduSwap

EduSwap is a futuristic, full-stack academic node designed specifically for the students of Govt. Polytechnic College Patiala. It aims to eliminate the fragmented process of finding academic results, syllabus, and resources by providing a centralized, decentralized-style "Terminal" portal.
